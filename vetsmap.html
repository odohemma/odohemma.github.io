Veterinary Clinics

Veterinary professionals carry out their practice in veterinary clinics. Veterinary professionals are saddled with administering veterinary treatment. Veterinary medicine deals with the prevention, diagnosis and treatment of disease, disorder and injury in non-human animals. Veterinary professionals monitor and control zoonotic diseases and food safety; maintain food supply through livestock health monitoring and treatment; and maintain mental health of animals (Wikipedia, 2018).
